About

EAT..AMORE..RIPETERE.. Buon Appetito!

We are a bunch of foodies, techies and entrepreneurs on a mission to introduce the joy of having wholesome & delicious homemade food to anyone, anywhere. On the one hand, we provide a platform for home chefs to share their passion for cooking with a much larger audience, and on the other hand, we also take the monotony out of regular food intake with a daily selection of fresh homemade meals delivered right to your doorstep.
